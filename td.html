Terrasse Drømmen

Visit the website

Website design and developement for Terrasse Drømmen

Terrasse Drømmen is a one man startup carpenter business based in Svendborg. My brief was to create a extremely simple one page website with only the most important informations for the customers and a logo. Since then Terrasse Drømmen has asked me to develope more things, such as a page with former carpentry projects (WIP) and designing three different prints for the customers, which to me indicate a happy customer. I have worked solo on the website, and I'm continuously going to maintain and update the website for Terrasse Drømmen in the future.
