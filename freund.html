FREUND KBH

Website redesign for FREUND KBH

FREUND KBH is a Copenhagen based, professional and succesful hairdresser. FREUND KBH asked me to develope a simple, responsive and functional website for their customers including bookinglink, pricelists, practical info, collab info, images of their work etc. Besides the website they asked me to redesign their logo and create formats of it for their social media presence. I have worked solo on the website, and I'm continuously going to maintain and update the website for FREUND KBH in the future.
